About Habit Tracker

What is Habit Tracker?

Habit Tracker is a simple yet powerful web application designed to help you build and maintain positive habits. Whether you want to exercise regularly, read more, meditate, or track your financial goals, our app makes it easy to monitor your progress and stay motivated.

Our Mission

We believe that small daily habits lead to big life changes. Our mission is to provide you with an intuitive, user-friendly tool that helps you establish positive routines and track your consistency with ease.

Key Features

Daily Habit Tracking: Log your habits every day and see your progress at a glance.
Monthly Progress: View your completion percentage for each month and identify patterns.
Customizable Habits: Add up to 11 custom habits that fit your lifestyle.
Secure Authentication: Your data is protected with secure login and password encryption.
Cross-Device Sync: Access your habits from any device with your account.
Light & Dark Theme: Choose your preferred theme for comfortable viewing.

How to Use

  1. Create an account with your email and password
  2. Add your daily habits (e.g., Exercise, Read, Meditate)
  3. Check off habits as you complete them each day
  4. View your monthly progress and stay motivated
  5. Edit your profile or password anytime

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Habit Tracker. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our web application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: First name, last name, email address, and username
  • Authentication Data: Passwords (encrypted and never stored in plain text)
  • Habit Data: Your custom habits and daily completion records
  • Usage Information: How you interact with our application

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate you securely when you log in
  • Store and retrieve your habit tracking data
  • Sync your data across multiple devices
  • Improve and optimize our service
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support

4. Data Security

Your security is important to us. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are encrypted using secure hashing algorithms
  • All data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore
  • Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL
  • We never store sensitive information like passwords in plain text or localStorage
  • Access to your data is restricted by authentication tokens

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored using Firebase services, which includes:

  • Firebase Authentication: Securely manages your login credentials
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores your habit data and user profile information
  • Data Backup: Firebase automatically backs up your data

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our application:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and hosting services

These services are bound by their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with:

  • Firebase (for storage and authentication)
  • Service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting business
  • When required by law or legal process

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data (contact support)
  • Opt-out of certain data collection practices
  • Request a copy of your data

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will securely delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

10. Children's Privacy

Habit Tracker is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information and terminate the child's account.

11. Cookies and Tracking

Our application uses:

  • localStorage: To store your preferences (theme, language) locally on your device
  • Session Tokens: To keep you logged in securely

These technologies do not track you across the web and are used only to enhance your user experience.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of our application after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@habittracker.com

Response Time: We will respond to your inquiries within 7 business days.

14. Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with relevant data protection regulations including GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) principles where applicable.
