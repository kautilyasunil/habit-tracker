Privacy Policy

Ye habit tracker app aapki daily habits ko track karne ke liye banayi gayi hai.

Data storage

  • LocalStorage: Aapka habit progress sirf aapke device ke browser ke localStorage me save hota hai.
  • Firebase: Agar future me login/sync feature add kiya jata hai, to data Firebase (Google) ke servers par store ho sakta hai.

Cookies, analytics, ads

Abhi app kisi bhi analytics ya ads ka use nahi karti. Agar future me Google Analytics ya ads add kiye jate hain, to unke through cookies aur tracking use ho sakti hai, jiska update is page par diya jayega.
